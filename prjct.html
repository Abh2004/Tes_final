1. Project Tree Census

In our lush green campus, the knowledge of position and usefulness of each tree can be very useful for the residents of campus. Project Tree Census by Technology Environment Society aims to create an online database and map so that any campus resident can access details (location, species, approximated height etc.) of all the flora in the campus. Student volunteers from National Social Service (NSS) also have agreed to collaborate for this project.

3. Roots of Freedom, 15 Aug 2022

On the occasion of the Independence Day, the Gaura Devi Herbal Garden was extended with 70 saplings which consist of 21 new species of medicinal plants. The tree plantation drive was in collaboration with other organizations nationwide (Team SHUNYA, IIT Bombay, NSS IIT Bombay, Sustainability Cell IITB, Social Service Club of IIT Guwahati, Students' Welfare Board of IIT Guwahati, and Rotaract Club of Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, Kolkata) to celebrate 75 years of independence of our country, with a similar event being organized on those respective campuses.

4. Environment Day Celebration, 05 Jun 2022

The president of Technology Students’ Gymkhana published a booklet named “Campus Herbs” based on the medicinal values of the plants in the Gaura Devi Herbal Garden inaugurated on the Earth’s Day. Then TES members along with other present students participated in an Anti-Plastic campaign to spread awareness about the harmful effects of using plastics and to reduce their usage as much as possible. Some cotton bags were gifted to the shops to encourage them to use biodegradable bags.

5. Earth Day Celebration, 22 Apr 2022

To celebrate the Earth Day on 22nd April, Gaura Devi Herbal Garden was inaugurated by the Director of IIT Kharagpur beside BC Roy Hospital. The motto of this Herbal Wellness Garden is to make the students and others aware of indigenous herbal plants and their tremendous medicinal values. TES student members created this garden to encourage the whole community to use the medicinal herbs for remedies in their daily life, gather knowledge about their benefits, and nurture the plants as well.
